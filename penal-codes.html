Weather conditions
Road surface conditions
Visibility conditions

Crimes Against the Sovereignty of the State

⚖️ Maximum Security Offenses

The most serious offenses that directly threaten or harm the sovereignty, security, and integrity of the state government and its institutions. These crimes carry the harshest penalties in the legal system.

FELONY High Treason

Capital crimes against state security including treason, terrorism, and espionage

MISDEMEANOR Public Order

Government impersonation, unlawful assembly, and similar offenses

INFRACTION Minor Offenses

Lesser violations against state authority and administrative processes
