Note: Speed limits may be reduced in specific areas such as school zones, construction zones, or during adverse weather conditions.

Basic Speed Law

Never drive faster than is safe for current conditions, regardless of the posted speed limit. Your speed should be adjusted for:

  • Traffic density and flow
  • Weather conditions (rain, fog, snow)
  • Road surface conditions
  • Visibility conditions
  • Pedestrian and bicycle traffic

Right-of-Way Rules

Intersections
  • Yield to vehicles already in the intersection
  • At 4-way stops, yield to the vehicle that arrived first
  • If vehicles arrive simultaneously, yield to the vehicle on your right
Special Right-of-Way
  • Emergency vehicles with sirens and lights
  • Pedestrians in crosswalks
  • School buses displaying flashing red lights
  • Construction/maintenance zones

Parking Regulations

Illegal Parking Locations:
  • Within 15 feet of a fire hydrant
  • In a disabled person parking space
  • On a crosswalk or sidewalk
  • In front of a driveway
  • In a tunnel or on a bridge
  • Next to red curbs
  • Double parking
  • In emergency vehicle zones
