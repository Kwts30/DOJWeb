Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission at the Department of Justice, Los Santos, is to uphold justice, protect the rights of all citizens, and ensure the fair and impartial application of the law within Los Santos. We are dedicated to fostering a safe and secure community through diligent investigation, prosecution, and the relentless pursuit of truth. We strive to maintain public trust by operating with the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and accountability.

Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of justice and a model of legal excellence for all of Los Santos. We envision a community where the rule of law is respected, where victims find redress, and where the guilty are held accountable, all while safeguarding the fundamental liberties of every individual. We aim to achieve this through continuous improvement, innovative legal strategies, and a steadfast commitment to serving the public with honor and distinction.

Departments

District Attorney's Office

Prosecutes criminal cases, upholds justice, and ensures fair trials for all. Works closely with law enforcement and the courts.

Judiciary

Presides over court proceedings, interprets the law, and ensures justice is served impartially and efficiently.

Public Attorney's Office

Provides legal defense for those unable to afford private counsel, ensuring everyone has access to justice.

Private Lawyers

Represents clients in civil and criminal matters, offering expert legal advice and advocacy in the justice system.

Our Subdepartments

Los Santos Police Department

Protects the city and its citizens, investigates crimes, and maintains order.

San Andreas State Marshal

Enforces state laws and maintains order within the state.

Federal Investigation Bureau

Enforces federal laws and maintains order within the state.

Los Santos Fire Department

Enforces fire safety and extinguishes fires.
