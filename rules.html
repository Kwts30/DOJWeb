Rules

Court Decorum and Professional Conduct

Essential standards for maintaining professional conduct and decorum within Los Santos courtrooms.

Courtroom Conduct:

  • Dress Code:

    Professional attire required: Business suits, formal wear, or official uniforms only

  • Behavior Standards:

    Maintain respectful demeanor, address court as "Your Honor", no disruptions

  • Electronic Devices:

    All devices must be silenced, no recording without permission

Professional Standards:

  • Punctuality:

    Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled proceedings

  • Communication:

    Clear, professional language; no argumentative behavior

Legal Process and Procedures

Standard procedures for conducting legal proceedings in Los Santos courts.

Case Filing Procedures:

  • Initial Filing:

    Submit all required documentation through official channels

    • Complaint or petition
    • Supporting evidence
    • Filing fees or fee waiver request
  • Service of Process:

    Proper notification to all involved parties within specified timeframes

Hearing Procedures:

  • Pre-Trial:

    Required conferences, motion hearings, and status updates

  • Trial Process:

    Structured presentation of evidence, testimony, and arguments

Evidence Handling Protocols

Standard protocols for handling, preserving, and presenting evidence in Los Santos courts.

Evidence Chain of Custody:

  • Documentation Requirements:
    • Record all evidence transfers
    • Maintain detailed custody logs
    • Document condition of evidence
  • Storage Procedures:

    Secure storage with controlled access and proper environmental conditions

Witness Testimony Guidelines

Procedures and guidelines for witness testimony in court proceedings.

Witness Procedures:

  • Oath Requirements:

    All witnesses must be sworn in before testimony

  • Testimony Rules:
    • Speak clearly and truthfully
    • Answer only questions asked
    • No speculation or hearsay

Document Requirements:
